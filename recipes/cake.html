Chocolate Cake

This is a chocolate cake and it is a good dessert. follow the steps and the ingrediants to make the cakes.

Ingrediants

  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened coca powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Frosting

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened chocolate powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. prepare cake pans
  3. mix dry ingrediants
  4. add wet ingrediants
  5. add boiling water
  6. bake the cakes
  7. cool the cakes
  8. make the Frosting
  9. frost the cakes
