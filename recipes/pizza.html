PIZZA

This pizza is topped with chicken and red onion. This pizza can be made very easily. Below is the Ingrediants and the directions to make the pizza.

Ingrediants

  • dough
  • BBQ Sauce
  • cooked BBQ chicken
  • Mozzarella
  • red onion
  • cliantro
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. pre heat the oven to 475C
  2. prepare the dough
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Add chicken
  5. add cheese
  6. add onions
  7. Bake for 30 mins
